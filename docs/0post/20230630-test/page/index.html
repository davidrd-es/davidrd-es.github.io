Long ago, when I wanted to enhance my Virtualization and Automation skills, I explored various options to acquire them beyond my daily work. Product documentation, trainings, and blogs were all helpful for taking initial steps. However, as I delved deeper into numerous subjects, I realized the need to personally test and retain my tests in an isolated environment. By this, I made the decision to purchase my first hardware to set up a homelab
